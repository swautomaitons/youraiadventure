Anthropic Said No to the Pentagon — What That Tells Us About Values

Anthropic turned down a Pentagon contract worth hundreds of millions. In a world where every AI company is chasing government money, saying no tells you everything about their values.

Key Takeaways

Anthropic declined a Department of Defense contract for AI-powered intelligence tools
02
The contract was worth an estimated $200-500M over 5 years
03
Anthropic cited their responsible scaling policy as the reason

Analysis

This decision will be studied in business schools. Anthropic walked away from what could have been their largest single contract because it conflicted with their stated values around AI safety.

Whether you agree with the decision or not, the signal is clear: they are building a company where values are not just marketing copy. When hundreds of millions of dollars are on the table and you still say no, that is real commitment.
