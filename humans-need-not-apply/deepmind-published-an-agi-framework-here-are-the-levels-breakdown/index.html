DeepMind Published an AGI Framework — Here Are the Levels

DeepMind just released a framework that measures how close we are to AGI. They define 6 levels, and we have already crossed Level 2.

Key Takeaways

Level 0: No AI. Level 1: Emerging (chatbots). Level 2: Competent (current LLMs)
02
Level 3: Expert. Level 4: Virtuoso. Level 5: Superhuman (full AGI)
03
DeepMind says we are solidly at Level 2 with some systems approaching Level 3
04
The framework measures both narrow and general task performance
05
This is the first major AI lab to publish a formal AGI measurement system

Analysis

DeepMind's framework is significant because it moves the AGI conversation from speculation to measurement. By defining clear levels with testable criteria, they have given the industry a shared vocabulary.

The fact that they place current systems at Level 2 (Competent) is both humbling and reassuring. We are further along than many skeptics claim, but the gap to Level 5 remains enormous.
